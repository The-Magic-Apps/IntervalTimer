Privacy Policy

The Magic Apps Interval Timer (“the app”) is provided by The Magic Apps. This policy explains how the app handles your data. In short: the app keeps your data on your device and does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

What the app stores

Your interval configurations and your run history (including any heart-rate data captured during a run) are stored locally on your device. This information is not sent to us and we have no access to it.

Heart rate & Health data

If you run an interval on a paired Apple Watch, the app reads your heart rate during the workout using Apple’s HealthKit so it can be shown live and saved alongside that run in your history on your device. Heart-rate data is used only for this purpose. It is never transmitted to The Magic Apps, and it is never used for advertising or shared with third parties. Health data is not written back to the Health app.

Subscriptions

Interval Timer Plus is an optional auto-renewing subscription sold through Apple’s App Store. Purchases are processed by Apple; we do not receive or store your payment details. Subscription handling is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Analytics & tracking

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Your control

Because your data lives on your device, you can remove it at any time: delete individual intervals or history entries, use “Delete All Data” in Settings, or delete the app.

Free tier and subscription

The app is free to use with up to two saved intervals. Interval Timer Plus is an optional auto-renewing subscription (£1.99 per year, or the equivalent in your local currency) that unlocks unlimited intervals and saving runs to your history.

  • Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your Apple Account is charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel the subscription in your Apple Account settings after purchase.

If your subscription lapses, the app remains usable on the free tier: your history is preserved (but new runs are not saved), and you keep two active intervals while any others become read-only.
